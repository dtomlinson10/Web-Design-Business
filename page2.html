What Are the Best Companies for Web Design and Why?

The best companies in the web design field include:

  • Razorfish
  • Deloitte Digital
  • 22 Squared

Razorfish

Razorfish is a technology based marketing firm that strives to use the newest and most advanced marketing techniques to reach the ever changing society. (Phillosophy94 (2013). Razorfish have worked with large comapnies such as McDonalds, Mercedes, Samsung, Special K, and many other big name clients. Razorfish was founded in 1997 in Seattle, Washington, USA as an agency developing websites and other digital marketing such as enterprise social media. They respond to client challenges with multi-channel marketing programs, that build business across the entire marketing eco-system, through their converged creative, media, technology and strategy model.

Deloitte Digital

Deloitte Digital is another web design business that competes with Razorfish and 22 Squared to provide the best design and implementation of websites for customers around the world. Deloitte Digital have worked with big companies globally to grow internatioanl business. They have offices in over 30 different countries including Australia, India and many countries accross Europe. Some companies, Deloitte Digital have worked with, include: Microsoft and other comapnies around the world.

22 Squared

22 Squared is a web design business that competes to a high standard within the web development field. The company is an agency which means the employees own the business collectively through selling stocks to employees similar to a limited company. This means that the team can work on the websites knowing the profits will be divided equally between all the employees. Therefore, the employees may be more motivated to create the websites and updates to the best of their ability.
